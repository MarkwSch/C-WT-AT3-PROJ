PYTHON TEXT ADVENTURE

For this task, we were required to develop a text-based adventure game using Python, following a set of guidelines. The game included a detailed world, interactions between characters, and management of objects. To ensure organization and efficiency, we adopted a modular and object-oriented approach, accompanied by thorough documentation and testing. Players were given specific instructions to follow in order to either win or lose the game, in line with the overall gameplay of text adventures.
